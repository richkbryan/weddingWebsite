How He Proposed

Richard told me he had made reservations for a fancy date night. To kill time we went for a walk and he took me to this pavilion. I had no interest and I told him no because I was hungry. Lol. He insisted that we should just check it out. Once we got under the pavilion, he grabbed my hand and led me to the roses. I thought the city of Holland did the decorations for Labor Day weekend lol. I even said "these are my favorite roses!". I was clueless. He proposed, I said yes (obviously) then he led me by the hand and took me behind the pavilion to where our families were waiting to surprise me. HE PLANNED EVERYTHING PERFECTLY! ❤
